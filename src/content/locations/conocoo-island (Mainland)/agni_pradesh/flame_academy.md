# Flame Academy

The Flame Academy is Agni Pradesh's premier educational institution, specializing in fire-based crafts, sciences, and martial arts. This imposing structure of red stone and heat-treated glass houses multiple workshops, laboratories, and training grounds where students learn to master and respect the power of flame.

## Academy Departments

- **Forge Mastery**: Training in blacksmithing, metallurgy, and weapon crafting
- **Pyromancy**: Study of fire prediction, control, and ceremonial uses
- **Culinary Arts**: Specializing in flame cooking techniques and heat-intensive food preparation
- **Glassblowing**: Creating art and functional items from molten glass
- **Flame Dancing**: Performance art combining dance with fire manipulation
- **Thermal Sciences**: Research into heat properties, volcanic activity, and energy generation

Students typically enroll for a five-year program, though shorter specialized courses are available for visitors. The academy's graduates are highly sought after throughout Conocoo Island for their expertise in heat management and fire-based crafts. The annual Exhibition of Flames showcases student work and attracts artisans and merchants from across the mainland.
