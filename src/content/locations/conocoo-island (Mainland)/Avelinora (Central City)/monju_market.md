# Monju Market

Monju Market is a vibrant Japanese-inspired marketplace that serves as the commercial heart of Avelinora Central City. With its distinctive red torii gates marking the entrance and lantern-lined pathways, this bustling market offers an immersive cultural experience that blends traditional Eastern aesthetics with fantasy elements.

## Features

- **Main Attraction**: Rows of colorful stalls selling exotic goods, magical ingredients, and artisanal crafts beneath strings of glowing paper lanterns
- **Historical Significance**: Founded by merchants from the eastern islands who brought their architectural style and trading traditions to Avelinora centuries ago
- **Natural Elements**: A central koi pond with a small shrine where visitors can make offerings for good fortune in their trades
- **Cultural Importance**: Host to the annual Lantern Festival where thousands of wishes written on floating lanterns illuminate the night sky
- **Visitor Experience**: Immersive sensory journey with the aromas of exotic spices, sounds of haggling merchants, and displays of rare monster-inspired crafts

Monju Market is most vibrant during the spring and summer months when seasonal festivals bring additional vendors and cultural performances to the already lively marketplace.
