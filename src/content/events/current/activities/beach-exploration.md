# Beach Exploration

The Beach is a special location that's only accessible during the Summer Festival. It's a perfect place to find water-type monsters and unique items.

## Location

The Beach can be accessed from the southern edge of town. Look for the colorful beach umbrellas and the sign that says "Festival Beach".

## Activities

### Monster Hunting

The Beach is home to several unique water-type monsters that can only be found during the Summer Festival. These include:

- **Wavecrest**: A rare Water/Light type monster that glows in the moonlight
- **Sandcastle**: An uncommon Ground/Water type monster that disguises itself as a sandcastle
- **Shellshock**: An uncommon Water type monster that lives in colorful shells
- **Tidepooler**: A common Water type monster that can be found in tide pools

### Shell Collecting

While exploring the Beach, you can collect special shells that can be traded for unique items at the Shell Exchange booth.

Shell rarities:
- Common: White Shells
- Uncommon: Pink Shells
- Rare: Spiral Shells
- Very Rare: Rainbow Shells

### Sandcastle Building

Participate in the daily Sandcastle Building contest for a chance to win special prizes. The contest is held every day at noon.

## Tips for Beach Exploration

1. Visit during different times of day for different monsters
2. Use Water Lures to increase your chances of finding rare water monsters
3. Bring a bucket to collect more shells
4. Don't forget to apply Sunscreen Lotion to avoid getting sunburned (which can temporarily lower your monster encounter rate)

## Rewards

Special rewards for Beach Exploration include:

- Exclusive Beach-themed monster accessories
- Special evolution items for water monsters
- Unique moves that can only be learned during the Summer Festival
- Beach-themed trainer outfits
