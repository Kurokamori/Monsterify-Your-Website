# April Fools Artwork Challenges

It's time for some lighthearted fun! Join us for our special April Fools event where you can earn the exclusive "Can't Believe It's Not Butter" item by completing silly drawing or writing challenges.

# Art Challenge Options :

- **Silly Drawing Challenge:** Create a humorous drawing of your trainer or monster in an absurd situation
- **Wacky Writing Prompt:** Write a short, funny story about your trainer encountering a ridiculous obstacle
- **Monster Meme Creation:** Create a meme featuring your monster(s) or trainers
- **Emoji Creation:** Create a sticker or emoji featuring your monster(s) or trainers

# Special Note :
The idea here is not so much about the quality of the art as it is the humor and fun. Take this as an opertunity to enjoy your monsters and trainers in a silly way.

# Rewards :
Complete any challenge to earn the exclusive "Can't Believe It's Not Butter" item, which can be used to... well, we're not entirely sure what it does, but it's definitely not butter!

Submit your artwork as you would normally, and then head over to 'prompt submissions' **Monthly Prompts** to submit your prompt. All 4 April Fools Prompts count as April Monthly Prompts, and you will also have the chance to recieve some other fun goodies along withthe monthly antique.
