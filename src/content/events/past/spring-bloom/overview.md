# Spring Bloom Festival

The Spring Bloom Festival was a celebration of renewal and growth in the world of Dusk and Dawn.

## Event Duration

The Spring Bloom Festival ran from March 1st to May 31st.

## Special Activities

During the Spring Bloom Festival, trainers participated in various special activities:

### Flower Planting

Trainers could plant special flower seeds in the Garden and nurture them to bloom. Different flowers attracted different types of monsters.

### Egg Hunt

Special eggs were hidden throughout the town. Trainers who found these eggs could hatch them to receive rare monsters.

### Butterfly Collection

Trainers could catch special butterfly monsters that only appeared during the Spring Bloom Festival.

## Special Monsters

The following special monsters were only available during the Spring Bloom Festival:

| Monster | Type | Rarity | Location |
|---------|------|--------|----------|
| Blossomite | Grass/Fairy | Rare | Garden |
| Nectarfly | Bug/Fairy | Very Rare | Flower Fields |
| Sproutling | Grass/Ground | Uncommon | Garden |
| Dewdrop | Water/Grass | Common | Morning Garden |

## Limited-Time Items

Special items that were available only during the Spring Bloom Festival:

- **Bloom Berries**: Increased the chance of finding Grass-type monsters
- **Pollen Powder**: Could be used to evolve certain Bug-type monsters
- **Flower Crowns**: Cosmetic items for your trainer profile
- **Spring Scrolls**: Contained recipes for spring-themed items

## Event Conclusion

The Spring Bloom Festival concluded with a grand Flower Parade where trainers showcased their most beautiful flower monsters. Prizes were awarded for the most vibrant and rare specimens.

This event will return next spring with new activities and monsters!
