# Type Changes - Attributes

Pokémon types define the essence of each creature, influencing strengths, weaknesses, and overall character. Below is an overview of each type, highlighting the traits and aesthetics that make them unique.

## Normal
Normal types represent the baseline—steady and unassuming.  
They are versatile and adaptable, often serving as a foundation with no extreme strengths or weaknesses beyond elemental matchups.

## Fire
Fire types blaze with intensity and passion.  
They are aggressive and dynamic, often depicted with vibrant reds and oranges that evoke heat, destruction, and renewal.

## Water
Water types embody fluidity and calm resilience.  
Associated with blues and cool tones, these Pokémon adapt to changing conditions like the ebb and flow of tides, bringing both soothing and formidable power.

## Grass
Grass types are in tune with nature, symbolizing growth and vitality.  
Their earthy greens and organic designs evoke a strong connection to the natural world, emphasizing healing and rejuvenation.

## Electric
Electric types are charged with energy and dynamism.  
They strike with speed and precision, their bright yellows and sparking motifs suggesting innovation, excitement, and unpredictable power.

## Ice
Ice types exude a crisp, frost-bitten aura.  
With a chill that can freeze opponents, they are characterized by cool blues and white highlights, symbolizing both beauty and a relentless, cold precision.

## Fighting
Fighting types are the epitome of physical prowess and determination.  
Their rugged, battle-hardened forms represent raw strength and discipline, with a focus on close combat and relentless endurance.

## Poison
Poison types radiate a dangerous allure.  
Often adorned in purples and greens, they carry an air of toxicity and mystery, able to slowly debilitate foes with their insidious effects.

## Ground
Ground types are firmly connected to the earth.  
They are dependable and robust, with designs inspired by rocky textures and earthen hues that emphasize stability and raw power.

## Flying
Flying types soar with grace and agility.  
Representing freedom and swift movement, their streamlined forms and light, airy aesthetics allow them to dominate aerial confrontations.

## Psychic
Psychic types harness mental strength and enigmatic energy.  
They evoke mystery and intellectual might, frequently using shades of purple and ethereal motifs to symbolize the unseen power of the mind.

## Bug
Bug types are small but tenacious, embodying the resilience of nature.  
They often surprise opponents with their persistent charm and intricate designs that reflect the diversity and adaptability found in the natural world.

## Rock
Rock types are steadfast and unyielding.  
Inspired by the enduring strength of mountains and stones, they project reliability and raw, ancient power through their rugged, earthy appearance.

## Ghost
Ghost types are shrouded in mystery and the supernatural.  
They bring an eerie, otherworldly presence to the battlefield, with spectral designs that evoke the unknown and a sense of haunting beauty.

## Dragon
Dragon types are legendary and awe-inspiring.  
They command respect with majestic and powerful designs, often rendered in bold colors that suggest both regal grace and fearsome might.

## Dark
Dark types are enigmatic and cunning.  
They evoke the shadowy side of conflict, with designs that blend mystery and mischief, reflecting a nuanced balance between malice and strategy.

## Steel
Steel types embody precision and resilience.  
Their metallic sheen and robust construction symbolize technological advancement and an unbreakable spirit, making them formidable in battle.

## Fairy
Fairy types exude charm, magic, and whimsy.  
Often depicted with soft pastel hues and delicate features, they bring an enchanting quality to combat, blending beauty with a surprising, hidden strength.
<br> But not all fairies are dainty and pink, some fairies are mischevious or even downright evil, inspired by older stories of elves and fae where they where cunning tricksters not to be messed with.
