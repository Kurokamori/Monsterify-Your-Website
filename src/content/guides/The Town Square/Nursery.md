## Requirements:

You need a standard egg to hatch egg  
Either Incubator or artwork required

Simple Hatch \= Roll Random Egg  
Complex Hatch \= Use items to modify hatch outcome.

Afterwards, select Mon(s) to register to your trainer.  
	Quantity of mons you can register is a default of 1 but more can be added using ‘DNA Splicers’
