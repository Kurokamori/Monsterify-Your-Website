# The Sub-Factions

Rangers are a massive organization, and as such have core roles within the faction that almost function as their own sub-factions. While Rangers do not officially recognize these divisions, they are a common enough occurrence that they may as well.

These sub-factions do not self-govern beyond role advocation within their own patrols, and are instead overseen by the faction leadership as a whole. That said, these sub-factions have certainly tried to self-organize, and now many have defacto leaders of their own, some with more authority than others.

# The Sub-Factions overall :

## The Hunters 
By far the most well organized and unique Sub-Faction these Rangers are focused on tracking down and neutralizing the most dangerous of monsters. They are the only Sub-Faction that is recognized by the faction leadership, and as such are the only ones to have official authority. They are uniquely not afraid to use lethal force when necessary, and get personally involved in the capture or neutralization of the monster.

The Hunters generally undergo special (though not technically recognized) training in the bootcamps, and are expected to craft their own weapons.

Some rangers resent them -- seeing them as monsterous for their willingness to intentionally harm or kill a monster.
