# Part 3 - And the Night Ends in a Bang

Illusions!
One way or another trainers start catching on. Their partners, friends, companions, are not them at all, but rather facsimiles. However they find out they now have a battle on their hands.

Before them are  Haranbur [fakemon] ready to fight for ruining their fun night.

Trainers can choose to fight them, flee from them, try to reason with them or any secret other option.

Many trainers resolve their own illusion and escape, but others may find themselves curious about what exactly this ball was. For those they can explore the hall and fend off varieties of monsters such as Freengi, Haranbur, Zorua, Zoroark, MirageGoagamon, Illoo and more.

At the heart of the ball, they’ll find the council of keepers. A cohort of illusion monsters, chiefed by an elegant Haranbur, who wish to see the drama of the night.

From them they can learn that the ball has been a long held tradition of these monsters, to bring trainers from around the world to a wonderful night of all things most wonderful. But these monsters, these keepers, are keepers of stories. And stories follow the most dramatic, most interesting, most intense. So unfortunately, the keepers lack some understanding of exactly how humans wish to spend this great night.

Once before the keepers, once more, trainers are faced with a choice, do they leave with the new information they’ve gathered? Or perhaps explain to the keepers their misunderstanding? Or has their wonderful night been ruined and they now have vengeance to enact?

Whatever the case, the wonderful night of festivities has come to an end.

## Prompts :

**Prompt 1 :**
Write or Draw the conclusion of your trainer’s illusionary conflict, however they find out that the illusion is fake (if they haven’t already) and what they do with the illusion once they know.

**Prompt 2 :**
If your trainer pursues the root cause of the ball : Write or Draw about their adventure through the now monster infested ball. Are they sneaky? Or perhaps they take on the monsters head on? Do they group up with other trainers or do they take it on alone?

**Prompt 3 :**
If your trainer finds the Keepers : Write or Draw about the trainer’s interaction with the keepers. Do they hear them out? Or attack on sight? How does it resolve?

**Bonus Prompt :**
The ball is now over, the illusions dispersed. Do your trainers know the people they met at the ball? And if so do they reach out to them? Do they try to meet up or perhaps simply send a message? Do they keep hidden to themselves the events of the night?

Write or Draw your trainer after the ball, and whatever it is they choose to do following the events of the night.
